About

Hi, my name is Nicolas Teixeira I am Brazilian born in Rio de Janeiro and currently living in San Diego California USA! I speak Portuguese as native language, English as a second language, and have an intermediate level of Spanish - with a goal to learn French! I’m a former soccer player that spent 12 years of my life playing in Brazil. I am also a Brazilian Marine Corps veteran – I served for over two years. After leaving the military, I started working in the Oil and Gas field as a Quality Control Inspector - ONSHORE and OFFSHORE. I worked for a multinational company called Weatherford which the headquarters is in Texas. My story in the US started in 2016, when I first came here to improve my English. The original idea was to master my English skills in order to pursue a college degree in the US. I have a passion for technology, and I am currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s in Computer Science at University of the People. The University of the People is a non-profit online University accredited by the Distance Education Accrediting Commission.
